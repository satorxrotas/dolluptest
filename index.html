About Daphne

"Beauty is only a blink away."

I'm Daphne — a Minneapolis-based multimedia makeup artist, hair stylist, and nail artist. I started doing this because I believe everyone deserves to feel like themselves, only more. Whether you're walking down the aisle, stepping onto a stage, or just want a reason to love your reflection, I'm here for all of it.

I offer hair, makeup, and nails under one roof — or wherever you are. On-site bridal, event glam, salon appointments, and makeup classes. Whatever the moment calls for, I'll show up ready.
